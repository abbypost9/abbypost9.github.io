About Abby Post

I’m Abby Post, a sophomore sports media and communications and marketing double major at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am a student intern at HuskerVision, the video production team with Husker Athletics. With HuskerVision I have been an EVS operator where I put together the college football updates. I also worked as a camera operator for the Nebraska Women’s basketball games. With HuskerVision I have learned the video production process. In the sports data and statistics class that I am currently taking, I have learned how to use data in RStudio to find statistics in various sports. These skills are preparing me for a future career in the sports industry.
